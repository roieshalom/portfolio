About

Hello! My name is Roie Shalom, I'm an experienced and creative UI designer with an eye for details and a desire for clear and coherent design. Fascinated by Innovation and technology, I made acquaintance with various technologies, from Laser cutting to vector based web design, from injection molding to selective UV lamination. In recent years I’ve been working as an interface designer and manager with both local and global companies such as HP, Stanley Black & Decker and SanDisk Corporation. For the last six years I've also been teaching design courses, disseminating the spark to others and I love it. I'm always happy to learn new things and meet interesting people so if you think we can create something new and amazing together send a message.
